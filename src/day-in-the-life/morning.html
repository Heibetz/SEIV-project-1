John’s Morning

Early Start

John wakes up at 5:00 AM to run and lift weights with his baseball teammates. After training, he comes home at 6:00, crashes back into bed for a quick nap, and wakes up again at 7:00.

Breakfast & Class

He gets ready for the day and makes time for breakfast before heading off to his 8:00 AM Principles of Management class.

Study Time

After class, John spends the rest of his morning working on homework and coding assignments until about 11:00, when it’s time for lunch.
